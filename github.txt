Once upon a time, a curious strand of hair wanted to know the secret of the tortoise’s calm.
Every morning, the hair would blow in the breeze, racing along fences and chasing sunlight.
The tortoise moved slowly and steadily through the garden, never hurried, never worried.
One day the hair asked, “How are you always so peaceful?”
The tortoise smiled and said, “I pay attention to each step, and I trust that little by little I will reach my goal.”
From that day on, the hair learned that speed is not the only way to win — patience and quiet courage are just as strong.
The end.
